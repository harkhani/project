Kedarnath

Nestled in the peaceful and pristine mountains of the Himalayas, lies Kedarnath. It is one of the most sacred and most visited Char Dhams, along with Badrinath, Gangotri, and Yamunotri. Holding a vital significance in the Hindu mythology, this place gets a lot of pilgrims who take the Char Dham yatra in order to purify their souls from their sins. According to the Hindu scriptures, Pandavas from Mahabharata went on the Char Dham yatra to wash away the sins they had committed during the war of Kurukshetra. By Swastika Mukhopadhyay

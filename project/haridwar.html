GANGA AARTI HARIDWAR- A SPRITUAL BEAUTY!

Visit Haridwar and witness Ganga Aarti & you will feel most alive! Haridwar in Uttrakhand is one of the holiest places to Hindus in India as it comes in one of the four places where the few drops of Amrit were sprinkled in the Ganga River and thus in every 12 years khumb mela held at Haridwar.It is the entry point to the four pilgrimages which we call “Chaar Dham Yatra” Badrinath, Kedarnath, Gangotri and Yamnotri. It is believed that Haridwar is known as trinity of holy gods: Brahma, Vishnu and Mahesh, literally the meaning of Haridwar is “Gateway of Gods”.
